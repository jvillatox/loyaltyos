# Campaigns (`@loyaltyos/campaigns`)

Campaign rules engine — create incentive campaigns with complex conditions without writing code.

## Campaign Types

- **Bonus Points** — extra points by period, category, or product
- **Spend & Get** — spend X, earn Y
- **Frequency** — visit N times in M days, get bonus
- **Milestone** — reach a spending level, unlock reward
- **Referral** — bring a friend, both earn
- **Birthday/Anniversary** — automatic campaign on special dates
- **Flash Sale** — short-window campaign (hours/days)
- **Tier Upgrade Bonus** — reward when member reaches a new tier

## Capabilities

- Budget capping
- Stacking rules (which campaigns can combine)
- A/B testing via `CampaignVariant` records
- Impact estimation before launch

See the [full README on GitHub](https://github.com/jvillatox/loyaltyos/blob/main/packages/campaigns/README.md) for details.
